The clinic

Built for the Balkans, by people who know both sides of the border.

Founded by Nina & Pape, Aevita Clinic brings German-grade blood-filtration medicine and physician-led IV therapy to Southeastern Europe at a fraction of Western prices.

Founders

Nina & Pape

Aevita was founded by a husband-and-wife team who spent two decades watching the same pattern repeat: Balkan patients flying to Vienna or Zurich for procedures their home region was not equipped to deliver, at prices their home region could not justify.

The plan was simple. Bring the German equipment in. License the German protocols in. Hire the best Macedonian physicians. Pass the cost arithmetic on to the patient. Add the medical-tourism logistics layer that EU clinics, frankly, do not bother with.

The clinic opened as a registered Private Health Institution (ПЗУ) under Macedonian law, with internal medicine, anaesthesiology and transfusion medicine on the licence. Every procedure is documented, every consumable traceable, every protocol on the wall.

Regulatory framework

ПЗУ — Private Health Institution

Aevita operates under Macedonian Ministry of Health regulation as a registered Private Health Institution (Приватна здравствена установа). Our licence covers:

  • Internal medicine outpatient practice
  • Transfusion medicine — blood handling and processing
  • Anaesthesiology — supervised sedation where required
  • Sterile procedure environment, documented protocols
  • Medical-waste contract with licensed disposal partner
  • Compliant with EU pharmaceutical-grade consumables
