Signature Procedure

EBO2 Blood Filtration

Extracorporeal blood oxygenation & ozonation

EBO2 is the premium end of medical ozone therapy. We draw your blood through a closed-loop circuit, filter out fat-soluble toxins and inflammatory debris, oxygenate and ozonate it, then return it. One session processes the equivalent of multiple traditional ozone sessions.

● 60–90 minutes ● EUR 850 – 1,000

MAH Ozone Therapy

Major autohaemotherapy — the classical ozone protocol

MAH (Major Autohaemotherapy) is the European clinical standard for medical ozone. We draw a defined volume of blood, expose it to a precisely dosed ozone-oxygen mixture, and reinfuse it. It is the most studied ozone modality with decades of clinical data.

● 30–45 minutes ● EUR 110 – 150
