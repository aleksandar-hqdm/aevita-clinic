Why Vlorë patients travel to Aevita

A longer corridor — most Vlorë patients fly Tirana–Skopje. We coordinate airport transfer and a recovery hotel as part of the international care package. For GLP-1 specifically, we recommend factoring in None — weekly self-administered injection of post-procedure observation before the return trip — covered in your patient package.

The arithmetic alone justifies the trip: comparable EU pricing for Medical Weight Loss runs EUR 350 – 600 per month. Aevita pricing is EUR 220 – 380 per month. The difference covers transport, accommodation and follow-up for most Vlorë patients with room to spare.
