Why Sofia patients travel to Aevita

Direct motorway corridor in three and a half hours, or 45-minute flight. We coordinate airport transfer and recovery accommodation for full-course bookings. For IV specifically, we recommend factoring in None of post-procedure observation before the return trip — covered in your patient package.
