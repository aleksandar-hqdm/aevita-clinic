Why Pristina patients travel to Aevita

Direct A4/E65 motorway, 90 minutes door-to-door. Many Pristina patients arrive in the morning, complete a single-session EBO2 with recovery, and are home for dinner. For IV specifically, we recommend factoring in None of post-procedure observation before the return trip — covered in your patient package.

The arithmetic alone justifies the trip: comparable EU pricing for IV Therapy Lounge runs EUR 150 – 600. Aevita pricing is EUR 90 – 420. The difference covers transport, accommodation and follow-up for most Pristina patients with room to spare.
