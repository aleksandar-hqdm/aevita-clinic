Travel & recovery from Budva

Coastal patients typically fly Tivat–Belgrade–Skopje. We work with a local concierge partner to handle ground logistics on both ends.

The clinic operates a complimentary medical concierge for patients traveling from Budva: we coordinate transfer, partner-hotel recovery rates and post-procedure follow-up by telemedicine after you return home.

For EBO2, MAH and NAD+ protocols we recommend a minimum 24-hour stay in Skopje for proper recovery before the return trip.

NAD+ Anti-Aging IV

Cellular energy & longevity infusion

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is the coenzyme your mitochondria run on. Levels fall measurably with age. Direct IV NAD+ bypasses the absorption bottleneck of oral precursors and rebuilds cellular fuel.

● 2–4 hours ● EUR 280 – 580 per session
