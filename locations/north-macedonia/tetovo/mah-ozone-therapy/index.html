MAH Ozone Therapy from Tetovo

MAH (Major Autohaemotherapy) is the European clinical standard for medical ozone. We draw a defined volume of blood, expose it to a precisely dosed ozone-oxygen mixture, and reinfuse it. It is the most studied ozone modality with decades of clinical data.

Why Tetovo patients travel to Aevita

45 minutes from the clinic by road. We offer a complimentary shuttle for booked patients on request and partner with Hotel Lirak for overnight stays. For MAH specifically, we recommend factoring in None of post-procedure observation before the return trip — covered in your patient package.

The arithmetic alone justifies the trip: comparable EU pricing for MAH Ozone Therapy runs EUR 180 – 250. Aevita pricing is EUR 110 – 150. The difference covers transport, accommodation and follow-up for most Tetovo patients with room to spare.
