Indications

Who this drip is built for

Every drip in the lounge is reviewed by your physician against your intake form and recent bloodwork. Immune Booster IV is most commonly booked for:

  • Pre-flight and pre-event immune loading
  • Acute upper-respiratory symptom onset
  • Post-viral recovery acceleration
  • Patients in high-exposure professions
