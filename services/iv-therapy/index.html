Frequently asked

Questions patients ask before booking IV

Do I need bloodwork first?
For most lounge drips a brief intake is enough. For NAD+ courses and Glutathione pushes we ask for recent bloodwork or run a basic panel at the clinic.
Can I combine drips?
Yes — your physician will sequence them safely. Common stacks: Myers' + Glutathione push, Vitamin C + Zinc, NAD+ followed by B-complex.
Is there a membership?
Yes. Monthly drip memberships and pre-paid courses carry a 15–20% saving over single drips. Ask your physician at intake.
